## The Technologies That Power Our Work

As a Senior Developer at Globoo Banking, I’ve had the privilege of working with cutting-edge technologies that push the boundaries of what’s possible in fintech. Here’s a glimpse into the tech stack and methodologies we use:

- **Backend Development**: We leverage **Node.js** and **TypeScript** to build microservices that are both performant and maintainable. Our APIs are designed with **GraphQL** for flexibility and efficiency, ensuring seamless communication between services.
- **Frontend Engineering**: The user experience is critical in banking, so we use **React** with **Next.js** to create fast, responsive, and accessible interfaces. Our design system is powered by **Tailwind CSS**, ensuring consistency and scalability.
- **Cloud Infrastructure**: Our systems run on **AWS**, utilizing services like **Lambda** for serverless computing, **DynamoDB** for low-latency data storage, and **S3** for secure file handling. We rely on **Terraform** for infrastructure as code, enabling us to manage and scale our resources with precision.
- **Security**: In fintech, security is non-negotiable. We implement **OAuth 2.0**, **JWT**, and advanced encryption protocols to safeguard user data. Regular penetration testing and compliance audits ensure we stay ahead of potential threats.
- **DevOps and CI/CD**: Automation is key to our workflow. With **GitHub Actions** and **Docker**, we’ve streamlined our CI/CD pipelines, allowing us to deploy features and fixes rapidly without compromising quality.
- **Data Engineering**: Handling financial data requires precision and speed. We use **Apache Kafka** for real-time event streaming and **Snowflake** for analytics, enabling us to process and analyze vast amounts of data efficiently.

## The Challenges That Shaped Me

Working in fintech is not for the faint of heart. The challenges are as complex as they are rewarding:

- **Scalability**: Designing systems that can handle peak loads during events like Black Friday or tax season requires meticulous planning and robust architecture.
- **Compliance**: Navigating the maze of financial regulations, from GDPR to PCI DSS, demands a deep understanding of both technology and legal frameworks.
- **Incident Management**: Despite our best efforts, outages happen. Leading a team through high-pressure situations, diagnosing root causes, and implementing long-term fixes has been a true test of my leadership and technical skills.
- **Innovation Under Constraints**: Balancing the need for innovation with the constraints of legacy systems is a constant challenge. It’s taught me the value of creative problem-solving and strategic thinking.
